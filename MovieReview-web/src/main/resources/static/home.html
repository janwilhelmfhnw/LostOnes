Featured Review by Mark B

Tenet (2020)


Memento is a masterwork. Interstellar is an unsung majesty but, unfortunately, fans of Christopher Nolan couldn't wrap their heads around the latter and voiced a vehement backlash when Tenet is actually the film that deserves such scorn. Nolan has gone and done it this time by making a film so monumentally stupid that it's laughable. If the laws of physics provide, then this film would invert itself back up his own orifice. What an absolute load of drivel. Not even good drivel. This is embarrassing for all involved. It'd be very easy to elaborate but I won't. The film doesn't deserve it. It's an incomprehensible disaster!
